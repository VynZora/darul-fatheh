Frequently asked questions

  • What age category is this event suitable for?
    Our events and courses are designed for various age groups, ranging from children (5-12 years) to adults. Please check the specific course details for age requirements.
  • Can I take pictures and film during the show?
    Photography and filming policies vary by event. Generally, personal photography is permitted, but professional recording requires prior permission from the administration.
  • Are there any known Health & Safety issues?
    We strictly adhere to all health and safety regulations. All our facilities are regularly sanitized, and we have first aid trained staff on site.
  • I'm unable to attend, can someone else attend on my behalf?
    Yes, tickets or course registrations are transferable in most cases. Please contact our support team at least 24 hours in advance to update the attendee details.
